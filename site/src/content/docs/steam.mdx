If you bought Guild Wars on **Steam**, the account has no separate email/password — Steam handles
the login. GW Launcher supports these as a special account type.

## Add a Steam account

In the Add/Edit Account window, tick **Steam Account**:

<LauncherPath kind="account" steps={['Add account', 'Steam Account']} />

With it ticked:

- The **Email** and **Pass** fields are no longer required.
- Set **Path** to the `Gw.exe` of your Steam installation.
- Give it a **Char** or **Title** so it's easy to spot in the list.

## Before you launch

<div class="note warn">

**Steam must already be running.** If Steam isn't open, the launch is aborted with a reminder to
start it first. Launch Steam, then double-click the account.

</div>

Everything else — [mods](/docs/mods/), [multi-boxing](/docs/multiboxing/), window renaming —
works the same as a regular account.
